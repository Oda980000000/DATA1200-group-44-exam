OsloMet and Pilestredet 35

This is a website for OsloMet Pilestredet 35. Here we will introduce what this website is about. We have been given the task of creating a website for Oslomet and specifically for building Pilestredet 35. This website is divided into four pages: Introduction page, topical page, accessibility test page and a page for pictures of OsloMet and Ellen Gleditsch. The topical page will mainly be about OsloMet Pilestredet 35 and Ellen Gleditsch. The accessibility test page is where we are evaluating digital products to ensure they can be used effectively by individuals with disabilities.

Some facts about Oslomet University

OsloMet — The metropolitan university is a university with its main campus in Oslo. It received university status in 2018 and was previously called the Oslo and Akershus University College (HiOA). The university offers a wide range of professional education. The main focus of the research is on the professions that are part of the university's study offer, as well as research on working life and professions in general. Besides the main campus in Pilestredet (Bislett) in Oslo, OsloMet has a campus at Kjeller in Skedsmo. Oslomet conducts research and education in the social sciences, health sciences, teacher training, technology, art and design.
